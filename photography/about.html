    About

Melissa Williams is the founder of “Will Snap”. She is an award-winning professional photographer based in Ipswich who delivers 'stand out from the crowd' photography, with service & quality all her clients are wowed by!

Renee has experience in all aspects of photography, from Portraits, Weddings, Head Shots through to Corporate & Commercial photography. She also specialises in “artsy” photography loving photography of semi-demolished or disused industrial buildings and street art.

Renee owns a studio space in Ipswich for head shots (branding, corporate, music) or portraits & has a mobile studio setup that can be used for commercial corporate work, head shots or portraits at home or on location. She normally travels to any location for weddings & portraits, covering Brisbane & South East Queensland
